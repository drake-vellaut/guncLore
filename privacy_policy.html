🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7/9/2025

🚫 No Cookies Used

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies on our website. Your visit is not tracked for advertising, marketing, or behavioral profiling purposes.

📊 Basic Visitor Analysis

To ensure the security and usability of our website, we use basic log analysis tools from a tracking image pixel on our godaddy site galacticgeographic[dot]gunc[dot]org provided by our hosting provider, GoDaddy. These tools include:

  • AWStats
  • Webalizer
  • GoatCounter

These tools analyze standard server logs to provide general insights such as:

  • Number of visitors
  • Pages visited
  • Referring websites
  • Browser and device types
  • Time and date of access

This information is used solely for:

  • Monitoring website performance
  • Identifying potential security issues
  • Improving user experience

We do not use this data to identify individual users, and we do not share it with third parties for marketing or advertising.

🔐 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the integrity and security of our website and any data collected through server logs. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored through these tools.
